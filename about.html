Our Story

We believe that nutrition is the foundation of health, yet often overlooked. People frequently seek medical attention without considering the impact of their dietary habits.
Meanwhile, nutritionists and dietitians face limited recognition and support. Students in the field often lack guidance on applying their knowledge in real-world scenarios. The Global Nutrition Community was established to address these gaps.

Our Achievements

  • 100+ members from India, Nigeria, Kenya, Benin Republic, and growing
  • Presence in over 8 universities worldwide
  • Mentorship program with experts in various niches
  • 10+ webinars and 5 workshops on topics relevant to nutrition professionals
  • 2 leadership trainings to empower our members
  • Team leads in each university to facilitate local engagement

Our Goals

  • Expand our global presence to 50 universities and 1000 members by the end of 2025
  • Establish partnerships with reputable health organizations to amplify our impact
  • Develop a comprehensive resource library for our members
  • Host an annual Global Nutrition Conference to bring our community together
