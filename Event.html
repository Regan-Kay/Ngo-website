Past and Future Events

Welcome to TGNC! We are excited to share some highlights from our past events and give you a glimpse into our future plans. Our community is dedicated to fostering growth, learning, and collaboration among nutritionists and dietitians. Here’s a look at what we’ve been up to and what’s coming next!

Our Past Events

  • Meal Planning Workshop: In our recent meal planning workshop, members learned the essentials of creating balanced and nutritious meal plans. This interactive session included live demonstrations, practical tips, and hands-on practice. Attendees walked away with valuable skills to enhance their professional practice and support their clients better.
  • Nutrition Education Webinar Series: We hosted a series of webinars focusing on different aspects of nutrition education, including life cycle nutrition, public health nutrition, and clinical nutrition. Expert speakers from the world industry shared their insights and experiences, providing our members with up-to-date information and practical advice.
  • Food Demonstration at 2 Maternity Centers: Our community organized a food demonstration at local maternity centers, where new mothers and lactating women learned how to prepare nutritious meals for themselves and their babies. This event highlighted the importance of proper nutrition during the critical postpartum period.
  • LinkedIn and Community Recipe Series: We launched a popular recipe series on the community platform, featuring healthy and delicious Nigerian meals. Each post included tips on retaining nutrients while cooking, making it easier for our community to enjoy wholesome meals.
  • New/Intending Members Training: We educate intending members about the benefits and opportunities of joining the community, highlighting the value of membership in achieving personal and professional growth.
  • Induction Ceremony: We formally inducted new members into the community with a networking, meet and greet session, and more.
  • Webinar: Finding Your Path: Should Early-Career Dietitians Pick a Niche? TGNC hosted a webinar that allowed fresh graduates, students, and others to have an extensive conversation on whether it is okay for early-career dietitians to pick a niche.
  • Webinar: The Role of Mindful Eating in Health and Wellness: In this insightful webinar, participants explored the principles of mindful eating and its impact on overall health and wellness. The session covered techniques for developing a mindful eating practice, understanding hunger and satiety cues, and making healthier food choices.
  • Workshop: Leadership through Volunteering: This dynamic workshop focused on the importance of leadership and community engagement through volunteering. Participants learned about the benefits of volunteer work, how to develop leadership skills, and the positive impact they can make in their communities.
  • Community Hangout in Lagos State: In the first quarter of this year, we organized a delightful community hangout in Lagos State. The event provided an excellent opportunity for members to connect in person, share experiences, and build stronger bonds.

Future Events

  • Advanced Nutrition Workshops: We are planning a series of advanced workshops covering topics such as sports nutrition, pediatric nutrition, and geriatric nutrition.
  • Virtual Networking Event: Our virtual networking event will bring together professionals from various sectors of nutrition and dietetics.
  • TGNC Annual Conference: Our annual conference will be a grand event featuring keynote speakers, panel discussions, and networking opportunities.
  • Community Outreach Programs: We are excited to launch several community outreach programs focusing on nutrition education and awareness.
  • Online Courses and Certifications: We will be introducing a range of online courses and certifications that members can access through our TGNC Online Library.
  • Mentorship Program: Our upcoming mentorship program will pair experienced nutritionists with new graduates and early-career professionals.
  • Community Hangout: We are excited to announce an upcoming community hangout in Lagos State!

Get Involved

We invite all members to participate actively in these events and take advantage of the opportunities TGNC offers. Stay tuned for more details on our upcoming events and how you can get involved. Together, we can continue to enhance our skills, expand our networks, and make a significant impact in the field of nutrition and dietetics. Thank you for being a part of TGNC. Let’s continue to grow and thrive together!
